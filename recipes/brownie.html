Oreo Stuffed Brownie

Description

An Oreo stuffed brownie is a rich and indulgent dessert that combines the fudgy decadence of brownies with the irresistible crunch of Oreo cookies hidden within. The brownies are made with a classic blend of chocolate, butter, sugar, eggs, and flour, while the surprise Oreo layer in the center adds a delightful contrast of textures and flavors. The result is a deliciously gooey treat with a creamy, crunchy Oreo filling, perfect for satisfying any sweet tooth.

  • 1 (18.25 ounce) package brownie mix
  • ⅔ cup of vegetable oil
  • 2 large eggs
  • ¼ cup of water
  • 12 chocolate sandwich cookies (such as Oreo)

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease 12 muffin cups or line with paper liners.
  2. Mix brownie mix, oil, eggs, and water together in a bowl until smooth. Spoon about 1 tablespoon batter into each muffin cup. Place 1 cookie into each muffin cup and top with more batter.
  3. Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted in the side of the brownie comes out clean, 20 to 23 minutes.
